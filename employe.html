CEO

Smith Dell
CEO
Since founding InComm in 1992, Smith Dell has been an early pioneer of the prepaid platform and point-of-sale activation technology. Under his leadership, what started as a company focused on the technology supporting the sale of long-lead distance phone cards has grown to be the leader of the prepaid technology industry.
